üí° What is the Feynman Technique?

Named after physicist Richard Feynman, this method helps you learn deeply by explaining concepts in simple terms. If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough!

üìñ The Four Steps

1
Choose a Concept
Pick a topic or concept you want to understand better.
2
Explain it Simply
Write it out as if you're teaching it to a child. Use simple language and avoid jargon.
3
Identify Gaps
Notice where you struggle to explain. These are your knowledge gaps.
4
Review & Simplify
Go back to your sources, fill the gaps, and simplify your explanation further.
